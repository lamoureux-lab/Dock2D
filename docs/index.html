DeepProteinDocking2D

A step towards predicting protein-protein interaction

Abstract

One of the long-term goals of computational structural biology is to explain and predict the phenotype of an organism from the basic physical and chemical properties of its molecular constituents. Given the central role of proteins in living processes and the fact that molecular function is strongly determined by three-dimensional molecular structure, a cornerstone problem in the field is to predict the structure of a protein from its amino acid sequence.
